How to Play

1. THE ARENA

Each match begins in a random Arena (like Jungle Fortress or Town Center). Every arena has a Type (Nature, Village, etc.) and exactly Three Attributes that count for that round.

2. SPECIALS

If your card's Type matches the Arena Type, your card's Special ability will activate automatically. Use this to gain an advantage!

3. WINNING

The player with the highest total in the three allowed attributes wins. The winner claims the loser's card! A player is out when they have no cards left.
