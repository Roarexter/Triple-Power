How to Play

1. ARENAS: Every match is fought in a random Arena (like Jungle Fortress) which picks 3 specific attributes (e.g., Strength, Speed, Agility).

2. SELECTION: Look at the Arena attributes and choose a card from your hand that has high points in those categories.

3. SPECIALS: If your card's Type (Nature/Village) matches the Arena's Type, your card's special ability activates!

4. WINNING: The player with the highest total points in the 3 attributes wins. The winner takes the loser's card forever!
